Investing should come after your emergency reserve, not before it.

## What to understand first

- The difference between saving and investing
- Risk versus return
- Why short-term money should stay liquid
- How compounding rewards consistency

## A student-friendly mindset

You do not need to start big. Learning how to set aside a small monthly amount matters more than trying to look advanced.

## Before any investment

Make sure you can cover:

- Monthly essentials
- Education expenses
- Family emergencies
- One month of personal fallback cash
