Part-time income can reduce financial stress a lot for students, especially when your class routine changes every semester.

## Good options for students

- Tuition
- Social media design
- Basic video editing
- Translation work
- Campus event photography

## Choose jobs with predictable payment

Tuition and retainer-style freelance work are often easier to budget around than random one-off gigs.

## Protect your study time

If a side income starts hurting class attendance or sleep, the money may not be worth the long-term cost.
