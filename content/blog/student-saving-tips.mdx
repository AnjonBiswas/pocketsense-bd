Student life in Bangladesh can make money disappear fast. The trick is not to become overly strict. The trick is to make cheap decisions feel normal.

## Start with your repeat expenses

Most students overspend in the same places every week:

- Tea and snacks between classes
- Ride costs on tired days
- Last-minute mobile recharge
- Weekend hangouts that quietly stack up

Track those first. If you control the repeat categories, your whole month improves.

## Try the 24-hour rule for impulse spending

If you want to buy something non-essential, wait one full day. Many purchases feel less urgent after a night of sleep.
